Wrangler: Greedy-Snake mechanism PLant

Wrangler was the first Plastic Antweight robot I ever built. When I traveled to Utah in May 2024 for the UCR Spring Fling, a couple friends and I agreed to throw three PLants into the ring as a multibot for the competition's PLeetle (Plastic Beetleweight) class. On the eve of the event I wasn't prepared very well: none of the plastic parts I'd printed fit together well, and the two-piece UHMW CAMs I'd made for ReBoot, even though they fit together well, were too wide for the holes in the boots.
