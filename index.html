About Me

Photo Credit: Ash Ponders

Hello! I'm Ella, a Robotics Engineering student at ASU.

I'm currently the Secretary of Combat Ready Robotics, a club which teaches ASU students to build their own combat robots. I've built several beetleweight (3lb weight class) robots in teams with other students, as well as one on my own (ReBoot), and one plastic antweight (1lb class). In my robot designs, I gravitate toward novel and interesting mechanisms, like ReBoot's CAM-and-slot-driven locomotion, Wrangler's "Greedy Snake" grabbing-and-lifting mechanism, and Saloon Sweeper's Bristle Drive.

I'm also currently working as a "Lifeguard" (Laboratory Space Assistant) at ASU's Interplanetary Initiative Lab.
