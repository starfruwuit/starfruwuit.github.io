ReBoot: CAM-driven combat robot


ReBoot is my beetleweight (3lb weight class) combat robot! It uses shufflers to move instead of wheels, and it has a double vertical spinner. I've been designing, competing with, and continually improving ReBoot since December 2023. Click here for the information on its performance at recent tournaments, as well as any tournaments it's signed up for in the near future!

The most complete section of this webpage is the one pertaining to version 3, as that is the most recent version, and the one I spent the most time working on.
